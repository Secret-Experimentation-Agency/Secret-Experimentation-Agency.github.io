Activities

The SEA's primary gimmick is the activities!
These activities vary in size, mechanics and rules.
There's not that many activities at the moment, but the staff is constantly working on adding new and engaging games.
Below, you'll find the activities currently available in the SEA, and additional information.


Mafia

Mafia is a social deduction game. Everyone receives a role, alligned with a team.
Every role has a special ability, useful for eliminating opposing teams.
Vote for someone each day, and the person with the most votes will get eliminated by the end of the cycle.
Roles vary for each game, along with teams and mechanics/twists.
